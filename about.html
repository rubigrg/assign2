Hello, I am Rubi Gurung and Welcome to my portfolio website. I am originally from Nepal but currently, living in UK to study well and to achieve my goals. I am studying Web Design & Development in Southampton Solent University as I want to be a web designer in the future and I am 20 years old. Before starting university, I went to Henry Beaufort School and Peter Symonds college in Winchester and studied BTEC IT and other subjects. I took a gap year before university as I was not ready to start university and in my gap year, I spent my time researching and findings of web design and practiced codings in HTML and CSS using Dreamweaver.


About Website Design Unit:

This unit is very important for Web Design and Development because this unit build on the skills accquired at Level 4 in: planning, design/design theory and technical skills to create modern responsive websites and apps. This unit covers semantic HTML 5 markup, presentation layer styling using CSS3, interactive behaviours with JavaScript, responsive and mobile first design methods, media queries and an introduction to frameworks such as Foundation and Bootstrap and an overview of pre-processing with SASS, Version Control with GIT and GitHub.

Southampton Solent University is located in Southampton, United Kingdom and has over 20,000 students. The main campus is located on East Park Terrace near the city centre and the students are represented by Solent Students' Union. Southampton Solent University's motto is "Spark Your Imagination".
